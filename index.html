Could your feedlot qualify for up to $1.8M in R&D co-funding?

Answer 8 quick questions to check your eligibility, then lodge your EOI directly from this page.

Question 1 of 80%
Question 1 of 8
What type of livestock operation do you run?
The current RAAD round is focused on beef feedlot operations.
Question 2 of 8
Is your feedlot NFAS accredited?
National Feedlot Accreditation Scheme (NFAS) accreditation is required to participate.
Question 3 of 8
How many head does your feedlot typically run?
A 2,000 head project over 2 years can attract up to $1.8M in MLA co-funding.
Question 4 of 8
Does your feedlot currently have shade or shelter infrastructure?
MLA gives higher priority to feedlots with no existing shade or shelter.
Question 5 of 8
Are you open to trialling a new or novel shade/shelter design?
RAAD favours fresh approaches — assessors prioritise projects that offer something new to the industry.
Question 6 of 8
Can you provide full feedlot closeout data?
Including animal performance, carcase grading and economic records — required for both Gold and Silver Standard.
Question 7 of 8
Would you share your research results with the broader feedlot industry?
This is a key assessment criterion — participants must share learnings so the whole industry benefits.
Question 8 of 8
Which research tier suits your operation?
Gold Standard ($2.46/head/day) — randomised controlled trial + peer-reviewed publication. Silver Standard ($1.97/head/day) — simpler commercial evaluation.
